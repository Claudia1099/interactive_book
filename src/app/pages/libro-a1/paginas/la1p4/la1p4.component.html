Clothes

We use the verb 'wear' to talk about the clothes someone has on.

Present simple form 'wear'
Example: She wears a uniform to school.

Past simple form 'wore'
Example: I wore a T-shirt and jeans yesterday.

Present continuous form 'wearing'
Example: He is wearing a shirt and tie.

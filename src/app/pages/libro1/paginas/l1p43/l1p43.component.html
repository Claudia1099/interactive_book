Grammar Flash

THERE IS / THERE ARE

There is: Existence (singular)
There is one fish in the fishbowl
There are: Existence (plural)
There are two fish in the tank

TEACHER: STUDENTS
There is a cat in the classroom There is a cat in the classroom
Two There are two cats in the classroom
Dogs There are two dogs in the classroom
One There is one (a) dog in the classroom
A rat
Two spiders
5 birds in the school
A teacher in the classroom
20 students
A cricket
EXAMPLE:

A. There is a rat in the room
B. Oh, my God, I hate rats
A. Don’t worry, I’ll kill it
B. Oh, you’re my hero.

A. There’s a puppy on the street
B. Oh, my God, I love puppies
A. Don’t worry, I’ll get it
B. Oh, you’re my hero.

Class 3

Yes / No Questions

Grammar flash
Teacher: Students:
s it a map? Affirmative Yes, it’s a map.
Is it a pencil? Negative No, it isn’t a pencil.
Are they sharpeners? Affirmative Yes, they are sharpeners.
Are they sheets of paper? Negative No, they aren’t sheets of paper
Is it a cell phone? Negative
Are they scissors? Affirmative
Is it a ruler? Affirmative
Are they glasses? Negative

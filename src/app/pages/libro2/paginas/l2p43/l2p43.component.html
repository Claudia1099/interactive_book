ORAL PRACTICE

What abilities and talents do you have?
Tell the class about some of the things you can do. Can you swim? Whistle? Play the piano? Cook?
Tell the class about some abilities or talents that you don’t have – things that you can’t do

    Example:
  • Student A: (…), can you play pool?
  • Student B: Yes, I can. OR: No, I can’t
  1. (…) wants to go to the zoo. Tell him/her what he/she can see at the zoo
  2. (…) wants to buy a hammer. Tell him/her where he/she can get a hammer. A wristwatch? Some bananas? Tennis Balls? A haircut?
  3. (…) has to go to the airport tomorrow. How can he/she get there?
  4. (…) is bored on weekends. Tell him/her some things he/she can do on weekends in (this city)
  5. (…) wants to go out to eat tonight. Where can he/she get a good meal?

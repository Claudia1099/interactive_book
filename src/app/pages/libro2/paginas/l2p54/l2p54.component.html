LISTENING ACTIVITY

Listen to your teacher read each sentence.
The pictures of Lazy Fab and his wife Pao are not in the correct order. Number the pictures in the correct order. Then write the number of the picture next to the sentences below. Finally, circle all the simple present tense verbs.

  1. a. Poor lazy Fab leaves the house and goes jogging.
  2. b. He lies down on the bench and says, “Finally, I am free!” Then he goes to sleep.
  3. c. Lazy Fab hates exercise. He wants to sleep, but he gets up. He puts on his clothes and sneakers with his eyes closed. Pao pushes him out of the house.
  4. d. Lazy Fab loves to sleep. He dreams about sleeping! But he snores all the time, and his wife gets no sleep. Pao is tired and needs to do something.
  5. e. He runs to the park and finds his favorite bench.
  6. f. Pao finds a way to get Lazy Fab out of bed. She wakes him at 6:45 every morning. He continues to sleep. She shakes him. She shouts in his ear, “Time to get up! You need your exercise, dear!”

Sometimes think and have are used in progressive tenses.

COMPARE:

I think that grammar is easy. ---- When think means believe, it is nonprogressive. I am thinking about grammar right now ----- When think expresses thoughts that are going through a person’s mind, it can be progressive. Tom has a car ---- When have expresses possession, it is not used in the present progressive. I’m having a good time. ---- In certain idiomatic expressions (e.g., have a good time), have can be used in the present progressive.

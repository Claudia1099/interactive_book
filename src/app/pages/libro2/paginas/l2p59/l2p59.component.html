Teacher ask these questions to the students.

Example:
Who cooks your meals?
My mother cooks my meals. OR My mother does
OR
I cook my meals. Or I do
  1. Who teaches you English?
  2. Who erases the blackboard in class?
  3. How many students in this class speak Spanish?
  4. How many students in this class come from Asia?
  5. Which gasoline costs more regular or unleaded?
  6. What cars get good gas mileage?
  7. What cities have a nice climate?
  8. What drinks contain caffeine?
ORAL PRACTICE WITH CAN – CAN´T

Answer the question. If the answer is no, add a statement with too + an adjective.

Example: Can you afford a new car?
Yes, I can
Or
No, I can’t. A new car is too expensive for me
  1. Can you wear a size 34 shoe?
  2. Can we go swimming outside today?
  3. Can we finish this book by next week?
  4. Can you read an English novel?
  5. Can you lift a piano?
  6. Can you walk to school?
  7. Can you study in the cafeteria?
  8. Can you have a party for fifty people in your house/apartment?
  9. Can you retire from your job?

Combine the two sentences into one sentence by using time clause. Punctuate carefully.

  1. First: I got home
    Then: I ate dinner

    • b) I ate dinner after I got home
  2. First: I washed dishes
    Then: I washed T.V

    • b) after

    Then: I left my apartment this morning

  4. First: I was eating dinner.
    Then: Jim came
    • a)While
    • b) while

    Then: I stood under a tree
    • a)When

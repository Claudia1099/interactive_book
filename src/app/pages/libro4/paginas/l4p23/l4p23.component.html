ACTIVITY 4.6

Practice pronouncing the following past forms of regular verbs.

GROUP A: Final –ed is pronounce /t/ after voiceless sounds
  1. looked
  2. asked
  3. helped
  4. laughed
  5. pushed
  6. watched
  7. dressed
  8. boxed
GROUP B: Final –ed is pronounced /d/ after voiced sounds
  1. sobbed
  2. believed
  3. filled
  4. poured
  5. roamed
  6. judged
  7. enjoyed
  8. dried
GROUP C: Final –ed is pronounced /id/ after –d and t
  1. needed
  2. defended
  3. added
  4. loaded
  5. waited
  6. rested
  7. counted
  8. halted
Underline the verbs and practice the following sentences aloud.
  1. My friend jumped up and down and shouted when she heard the news.
  2. The concert lasted for two hours.
  3. With the coming of spring, the river flooded and inundated several villages.
  4. She tapped the top of her desk.
  5. He described his house.
  6. They demanded to know the answer.
  7. The airplane departed at six and landed at eight.
  8. Alice pushed and I pulled.
  9. He handed me his dictionary.
  10. Jack tooted his horn.
  11. They asked us to help them.

CHAT TIME

  • A. What did you do last weekend?
  • B. I travelled by car to the beach.
  • A. Who did you go with?
  • B. I went with my friends.
  • A. What did you do last vacation?
  • B. My family and I travelled to Miami.
  • A. Where did you stay?
  • B. We stayed in a hotel.
Now, ask your partner for her/his activity during the last weekend or vacation.

IRISH STEW
  1. Cut 2 pounds of beef into small pieces.
  2. Cut up 6 potatoes.
  3. Put 3 teaspoons of oil in a pan and heat it.
  4. Put the meat into the pan and cook it for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the potatoes to the meat.
  6. Cook them together for 5 minutes.
  1. While the meat and potatoes are cooking, cut up a large onion.
  2. Add onion to the meat and let everything get brown.
  3. Add 2 cups of water and cook the stew for 2 hours.
  4. Add a cup of corn and two tablespoons of flour.
  5. When the stew is finished, make a pound of pasta.
  6. Serve the stew on the pasta.

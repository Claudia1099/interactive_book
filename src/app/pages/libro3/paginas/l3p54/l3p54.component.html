ORAL PRACTICE

Example: 1. Are you going to go out for dinner this evening?
Follow-up question: Where are you going to have dinner?
When everyone has finished writing, go through the follow-up questions with the class

Divide the students into pairs.

  • Students then take it in turns to ask their partner about their future plans using the questions on their worksheet.

Read each question in the first column and then write a follow-up question using 'going to' in the third column. Then, ask a partner about their future plans and write down their answers.

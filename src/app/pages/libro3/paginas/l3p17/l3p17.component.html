ORAL PRACTICE

Now, work in pairs (Student A and B). Test your partner on the object pronouns. Student A turns over their worksheet, so they can't see the sentences and answers. Student B reads a sentence from the worksheet.

Student A then reads the sentence back to Student B, adding in the correct object pronoun. After 10 sentences, swap roles. Score yourself one point for each sentence you get right.
